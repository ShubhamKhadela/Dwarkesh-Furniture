About Dwarkesh Furnishing

Dwarkesh Furnishing specializes in premium PVC and wooden furniture solutions for homes, restaurants, offices, shops, and commercial spaces.

We focus on functional, durable, and elegant furniture that enhances both aesthetics and usability. From complete home furniture setups to customized commercial furniture installations, we provide tailored solutions designed to match client requirements and space utilization.

Our commitment is to deliver quality craftsmanship, practical design, and long-lasting furniture solutions.
